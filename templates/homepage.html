Explore the beauty and importance of forests with these facts:

  • Forests cover about 31% of the total land area on Earth.
  • Forests are home to over 80% of the world's terrestrial biodiversity.
  • They play a crucial role in mitigating climate change by absorbing carbon dioxide.
  • Forests provide habitat, food, and resources for countless species, including humans.
